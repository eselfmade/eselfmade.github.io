How to Verify Kyc?

Follow these steps to get your Kyc verified.

1. Login to your User Dashboard https://eselfmade.in/user.sm
2. Tap Bank under Account
3. Enter your IFSC Code, Account Number and PAN Number and tap Upload
Once you've entered IFSC code, the Bank Name and Branch name will be automatically fetched
4. Tap KYC under Account
5. Upload your Bank Pass Book photo and PAN Card photo
Your details will be verified within one working day. Usually it'll be verified in few hours
You can check the status of the KYC verification in the User Dashboard
If your KYC is verified, no alert will be displayed on your dashboard
If your KYC is not verified, the error details will be displayed on your dashboard
