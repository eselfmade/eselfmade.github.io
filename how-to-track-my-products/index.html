How to Track my Products?

Follow these steps to Track the Products purchased

1. Login to your User Dashboard https://eselfmade.in/user.sm
2. Tap Purchase History under Products or visit https://eselfmade.in/user.sm#/products/history
3. Your entire Purchase Histories will be listed along with the Status, Courier, Dispatch mode and Tracking ID (If dispatched)
4. Tap on the Tracking ID to track the current status of the Products
Tap the + icon to expand
If the Status is Pending, then the products are not yet dispatched from our end.
