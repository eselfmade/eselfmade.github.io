How to Reset Selfmade Password?

Follow these steps to change your Selfmade password

1. Goto https://selfmade4u.com
2. Tap the User Icon at the top
3. Tap the Forgot Password
4. Fill your SMID, Mobile Number and Email in the Forgot Password Form and tap Forgot
If your details matches, you'll get this response
If your details doesn't matches, you'll get this response
5. In the email you've received, tap the Reset Password Button
Note: This link will be valid only for 15 Minutes
6. Fill the Password Reset Form with correct data and tap Reset
Note: If you haven't configured for TOTP, you can leave the TOTP field as blank
If your details matches, you'll get this response
You can now login with your new Password at https://selfmade4u.com/login.sm
